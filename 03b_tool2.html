Character Details

What is Character Detail

Characters has multiple attributes connecter to them such as name, type of weapon used, elemental power they possessed, talent materials and their region.

All characters may have a different material used with each other to level up or ascend further. But, some of them may share the same materials which will confuse players who to build up first.

By referring to a simplified detail, players got to plan out who to build first and will be able to enjoy their game without making a mess or level up too slowly

How To Use
