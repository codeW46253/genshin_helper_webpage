Game Information

Genshin is a game produced by the company Hoyoverse, previously known as MiHoyo. A Chinese game company located in Shanghai, China.

Genshin Impact was released on 28 September 2020 in Android, iOS, PS4 and Windows, 28 April 2020 for PS5 and 20 November 2024 for Xbox Series X/S.

Genshin Impact starts its development in 2017 while taking inspiration from The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, several anime scenes, Gnosticism and some other games while representing a wide array of cultures around the world including world mythology.

Genshin Impact promote players with open-world adventure, unique elemental-based combat mechanism, animation styles, real life cultural-base referenced character design and a wide range of musical composition from classical, jazz, rock, traditional, poetry and Electronic Dance Music (EDM).
