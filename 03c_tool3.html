Material Calculator

What Is Material Drops

Material drops are items gathered from every domains and item collected after defeating a Boss Enemy or Weekly Bosses.

The amount of items collectible from domain are static and changed depends on domain level while drops from normal Boss type enemies are change depends on World Level.
