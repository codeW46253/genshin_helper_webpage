Time Manager

What Is Time Management?

Time management in terms of in-game materials gathering refers to strategically planning and optimizing how you spend your time within the game to efficiently collect the resources needed for character progression, crafting, or other in-game activities.

In Genshin Impact, some materials need a special item to collect. This item is called a resin, which regenerates over time. Initially, there are 3 types of resins:

Original Resin This resin regenerates at a rate of 1 resin every 8 minutes. Players can accumulate up to 200 Original Resin at a time, but it takes about 26 hours and 40 minutes for a full replenishment from 0 to 200.
Fragile Resin This resin is collectible from events, the Battle Pass (Gnostic Hymn), and Adventure Rank level up. This resin can restore 60 Original Resin in one use.
Weekly Special Resin (Transient Resin) This resin also provides a bonus of 60 Original Resin in a single use. However, this resin is only accessible for players who unlock a special region inside the Serenitea Pot after completing the "A Teapot to Call Home" quest.

Time Managing Tool

How To Use

This website requires users to create their weekly planning by selecting the items they need to gather each day. Since each domain changes its collectible materials, players need to plan their time to build up their character's weapons, talents, and levels.

To use the tool, just select the items you need to collect each day by referring to the Character Talent's Material list.
